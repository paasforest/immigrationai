## üìù Voice-Over Tips

### Tone & Pace
- **Friendly but professional** - You're helping, not selling
- **Moderate pace** - Not too fast, allow viewers to follow along
- **Enthusiastic but calm** - Show excitement without being pushy
- **Clear pronunciation** - Especially important terms like "Schengen", "eligibility"

### Emphasis Points
- Emphasize **time savings** ("2 minutes", "in minutes")
- Emphasize **ease** ("no stress", "effortless", "simply click")
- Emphasize **professionalism** ("professional", "compelling", "bulletproof")
- Emphasize **results** ("get results", "strengthen your application")

### Pauses
- Pause after showing each major action (clicking buttons, filling forms)
- Pause when showing results (generated documents)
- Pause before important statements ("This is crucial...")

### Background Music (Optional)
- Light, upbeat instrumental music
- Low volume - voice should be clear
- No lyrics - avoid distraction
